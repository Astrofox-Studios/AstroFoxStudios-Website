Destiny Games Tester 4

A Special Mini Event for Destiny Games

As we approach the final stages of development for Destiny Games, we're thrilled to announce "Tester4", our fourth tester event. This mini halfway event is designed to bring together our dedicated player roster, new participants, our tester team, and some of our staff for a fun, collaborative testing experience. Unlike our usual test events, "Tester 4" lets players play a test without worrying about penalties for being testers. It's also a fantastic opportunity for content creators to stream and create content while helping us identify bugs and areas for improvement.

Event Date and Participation Details:

Tester 4 will take place on the 14th of September 2024 at 8 PM BST. If you want to join in, you can find the participation form on the Astro Fox Studios website at https://astrofoxstudios.com. The staff sign-up form is also available there if you're interested in becoming a part of the team. Please note that signups will close on the 4th of September 2024 to allow us time to prepare.

Event Structure:

Tester 4 will feature four games: Speed Through Space, Void Hunters, Cosmic Combat, and Wipeout Rush. As a special treat, Capture the Flag will be the final game of the event where the top 2 teams will face off! Players will be grouped into eight teams of five, assembled by our team-making staff. Throughout the event, participants will have the chance to vote for their preferred games in the selection sphere.

Content Creation and Community Involvement:

We're encouraging content creators to get involved by streaming or making videos about Tester 4. However, it's important to clarify that this is a test event, so bugs and issues are expected. This is a fantastic opportunity to showcase Destiny Games, and we're eager to see what the community creates.

Feedback and Support:

To ensure the continued improvement of Destiny Games, we're setting up a dedicated Discord server specifically for Tester 4 Participants. There will be a feedback channel where players can share their thoughts, report bugs, and suggest improvements. Additionally, if you need more direct support, you can open a ticket in the public Astro Fox Studios Discord server.

Participation Limits:

To maintain a focused and effective testing environment, player participation will be capped at 40 players, divided into eight teams of five. This setup Will be the default for all events.

Looking Forward:

After three years of development, we're incredibly proud of how far Destiny Games has come, and "Tester4" is our way of showing off all the hard work that's gone into making it something special. We're beyond excited and deeply grateful for the ongoing support from our community. Join us in shaping the future of Destiny Games!

What's Next for Astro Fox Studios?

While Destiny Games is a major focus for us at Astrofox Studios, it's not the only project we're working on. We're also developing EXP Host, our Minecraft server hosting service that offers affordable hosting options, including the platform where Destiny Games itself runs. Additionally, we're working on Tropiclash, a tropical-themed MMORPG-style Minecraft server that's currently in development and promises a unique gaming experience. As for Destiny Games, we're aiming to hold our first full-scale event by the end of the year. With a massive recode behind us, we're confident in delivering something truly special. Stay tuned—there's so much more to come!
