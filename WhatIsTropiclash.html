Tropiclash

What is Tropiclash?

Tropiclash is an exciting public Minecraft server filled with custom minigames, full games, and theatrical live events. Blending creative concepts from its sister server, Destiny Games, Tropiclash brings unique gameplay experiences to players, offering an evolving and dynamic world like no other. It is the first server to introduce so many custom live events, ensuring that players are always engaged and immersed in new adventures.

Your Raft

In Tropiclash, players will experience a boating feature that centers around a customizable raft. This raft isn’t just a personal hub—it’s your floating farm and resource generator. Players can expand their rafts to include large-scale farms, inspiring a grind to create massive, profitable farming operations. Specific crop plots are required for growing both vanilla and unique crops, adding a layer of strategy to farming.

As you progress, you’ll unlock upgrades for your farm, including automated systems and other exciting features still in development. Farming efficiency is dynamic—online players will see faster crop growth, while those offline will experience slower progression. To encourage active gameplay, participating in Tropiclash’s minigames will generate more revenue than passive farming, providing a balance between farming and active engagement.

In addition, players will be able to upgrade skills, offering a competitive edge as they climb leaderboards and showcase their dominance in various aspects of the game. A marketplace will allow players to trade and earn, and furniture items can be bought to customize your boat, ensuring that your raft is as stylish as it is functional.

The Storyline

Tropiclash exists in a universe where powerful forces, mysterious events, and deep connections shape the world. The game’s storyline is rich with hidden secrets, ancient powers, and fractures in reality that link different worlds. As you explore the island, you’ll uncover fragments of a much larger story that spans multiple realities.

Key factions and characters play pivotal roles in the unfolding drama, but their true motives remain shrouded in mystery. As you progress through the game and participate in live events, you’ll get to experience the evolving narrative and discover how your actions impact the world.

Explore Tropiclash Island

Your adventure begins on Tropiclash Island, a mystical land formed at the dawn of the universe. The island is connected to other unique and unexplored territories like Otter Outpost, Rocketeers Refuge, and Skull Island, all linked by a train system that makes travel easy and exciting. Beneath the surface lies the Crystal Caverns, a magical place where you can upgrade your gear, tackle boss battles, and uncover the island’s secrets.

Join the Adventure

Tropiclash is still in development, with testing estimated to begin in January 2025, and the server expected to exit early access around summer 2025. As we continue to build this unique experience, you can look forward to a multitude of exciting features and a rich gameplay experience.

Players can also expect to see a variety of games ported over from Destiny Games, our Minecraft event. These will bring an additional layer of fun and competition to Tropiclash. For more information, visit Destiny Games.
